Animal Vassal

You are property of an Animal Patron, but crave a human life. You know better than to displease your liege.

Sample Names

  • Sapp
  • minnred
  • Homis
  • Wammel

You Start With:

  • Dagger (d6, decorated to suit your patron).
  • Ridiculous Garment marking you as property of an animal.

Which Animal Patron Did You Serve?

   
£2 The Bear Parliament: Seemingly no intelligence beyond normal bears, but they know that they own you.
£4 A Mummified Platypus: Can communicate telepathically. Mostly desires food that it cannot eat.
£6 A Big Old Evil Snake: Talks very slowly and weaves complex plots that never amount to anything.
£8 Sea Turtle: Stuck in a tank somewhere. Speaks through a child translator.
£10 A Talking Emu: Eloquent but stupid. Always carries their Insubordination Rod (d8).
£12 Giant Mole: Delivers typed instructions, then burrows back underground.

What Errand Have You Abandoned?

   
1HP Capturing a Deserter: Take a Blowgun and 3 Tranquilizer Darts (DEX Save to avoid sedation).
2HP Hunting Down Somebody Know For Eating Your Patron’s Species: Take a Pistol (d6).
3HP Retrieving a Specialist Food That They Love: Take One Piece of Fine Food suitable for your patron.
4HP Buying Fine Clothes For Your Patron: Take a Formal Suit to fit them.
5HP Delivering a Declaration of War to an Old Rival: Take a Ceremonial Bugle.
6HP Punitive Pilgrimage to Repent For a Past Mistake: You are missing one eye.

Dark Tower XIII

Date: February 14th, 2026

Players: Gary, Hayley


Characters

Gary played Gabel the Toothbearer.

Hayley played Jillian, Acolyte of Hecate, and Killian the Wizard's Apprentice.

Summary

  • Travelled to Mithras' Cross, Met Vani at her inn. Bentley slumbering, dwarves discussing.
  • Travelled up to the Shrine of the Weeping Maiden. Fought lizards who poisoned Killian and Gabel, turning the lips grey. Asked the Maiden Two questions, became suspicious of a scam.
  • The next morning, Jillian healed Bentley with magic of Hecate. Hecate agreed to confront the scammer in the shrine.
  • Chanting skeletons behind curtain in shrine. Fought skeletons.
  • Descended to true shrine, saw corpses. Met Stone Giant and made trade for information.
  • Answered riddle from celestial beaver Custos Kastor, granted Gabel Mithras' teeth, and the right to see the maiden.
  • Summoned Lion People using the teeth of the dead between Set temple to south. Battled statues in Sets palace. Passes a Hot Pool, Cold Pool, Smelly Pool trap.
  • Vanquished the Blind Demon Mahna, the Runt of Set.
  • Took Set's Fang, Saw Vision of Dark Tower Wrapped in Snake. Jillian got Huge Snake Bite Scar.
  • Gabel destroyed Set's Fang with Mitra's Teeth.
  • Paid Stone Giant to Destroy Fake Temple and Restore the original shrine to the weeping maiden.

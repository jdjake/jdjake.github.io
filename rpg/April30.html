Taking The Ship Back To Port

Date: April 30th, 2024

Players: Abe, Dan, Gary, Hugh


Characters

Abe played Bep 'Gunpowder' Glover, a Jesuit Monk based on the isle of Yuma. Known for his stench, the rotten teeth hanging out of his mouth, and his preference for alchohol.

Dan played Iago ‘Le Red Bird’, a backstabbing rapscallion, a sailor with an insatiable urge for treasure. His previous adventures lead him to a narrow escape from a cannibalistic ending, but he never escaped that smell.

Gary played Annemicke 'Flatline' Hollegate, a known pirate and heretic amongst the colonial powers of the Caribbean. He'd gained a knack for wilderness survival, even if it'd cost him his right eye in the process.

Hugh played Low Roller , a swashbuckler who concealed a mysterious past as a barkeep with a liking for mocking banter amongst the other crewmates. There was something contagious about him.

BLAH

  • You recovered the Meteor on board _The Whispering Serenity_.
  • Parash is infected with something nasty from one of the reanimated corpses on the ship. Managed to find a tincture to temporarily alleviate the effects, but need to search for a more permanent cure.
  • Made your way down to Port Fortune, to rendezvous with an employee of Benjamin Cavisham. You arrived in the evening and spent the night cajoling at the Sultry Siren, waking up in the Pig Sty with an unknown amount of silver.
  • Benjamin Cavisham is staying at the Chaste Mermaid, in a less fun part of town.

Echoes in Grease and Blood

Date: May 6th, 2025

Players: Avi, Diego, Eric, Liam


Characters

Avi played Ru'in

Diego played Ahkesh

Eric played Asseyn

Liam played Aodh .

Summary

In Derelictus, the city between, an old, suspicious looking tech-savvy man smelling like metal and grease named Varicus approaches the adventurers as they prepare to travel down an old maintainance shaft of the Vermissian train tunnels below. He knowns the Vermissian handshake, which wins Ahkesh's trust. He tells the group he built a perfect copy of himself, a mechanical man, which, upon activation, ran off in fear down the tunnels. He offered the group knowledge and information on the heart in return for recovering the mechanical copy.

Ru'in and Aodh tapped on his head to see if he was actually the mechanical clone. This threw Jacob off, and he left to rewrite the entire campaign (to let Annabel in to the house). Varicus was human, and was offended that we suspected him.

The adventurers descend into the maintainance tunnels in a hand-car using a lever pump.

The tunnels were covered in maps and writing on the wall which seemed like nonsense, scribbled in engine oil and blood. Asseyn discerned the occult writing, and used the cryptic maps to discern the direction to travel through the maintainance tunnels.

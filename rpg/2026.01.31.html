Dark Tower XI

Date: January 31, 2026

Players: Abe, Avi, Gary, Liam


Characters

Abe played Armalas, Malloc-Sworn.

Avi played Nails the Halfling Thief, and Malach the Blackguard.

Gary played Titus the Mighty, and Tigerius the Wizard.

Liam played Robbi and Bobbi Hobbington, Halfling Knaves of Legend.

Summary

  • Fought Henchmen.
  • Fought Apprentices Coming From Downstairs.
  • Found Secret Ruby. Orson's Disguise Broke, and he Confronted You About It, And Then Vanished.
  • Saw Hole Down to Writhing Snakelike Forms.
  • Saw Beareagle Nest and Guano Covered Wooden Bridge.
  • Went Downstairs, Explored Laboratory with Huge Caged Ape.
  • Explosion Trap in Desk. Found Invisible Daggers and Invisible Box the Size of Four Matchboxes, with Round Domed Top.
  • Left Laboratory, Fought One Minotaur. Robbi Ran Reconnoisance to the East, Saw Two Patrolling Minotaurs and a Wall that observed all Light. Back in the Lab, the Ape began casting spells, transforming into a Writhing Form as the party ran back upstairs.
  • Armalas Went South, Dodged Some Mould Horrible Smelling Men, Saw a Deal Being Made Between a Cloaked Figure and More Mould Men, Then Retreated to a Deadend. As He Was Swarmed, and Skelly was Felled, He Found an Illusory Wall and Escaped the Mould Men.
